ABOUT SANGAGMAM

GREAT OPPORTUNITY

SOCIALIZE

Sangamam is the unity of arts. The Team Sangamam consists of 20 clubs to explore the extra-curricular activities of students and to enrich their skills. During this Covid-19 pandemic situation, KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology students are conducting online webinars and various sorts of events for school and college students. E-certificates are provided for both the internal and external participants as a token of active participation.

Learning beyond the academics also play a major role in some aspects. Everyone should explore and reveal their talents out. Sangamam creates a nice platform for the students to expose their skills and innovation in extracurricular activities.

Each club in Sangamam are conducting webinar with a well talented and experienced resource person. It paves the way to feed the participants with additional knowledge and lifetime experience which was shared by the speaker. Many positive feedbacks were received for the conducted webinars.

The contest for school and college students are quite interesting and more number of responses were received. We received good feedback from the parents and also they requested to extend the duration of contest. It creates an opportunity for the students to find their own talents and to enrich their skills.

Let us all don’t stop growing our skills and talents.

Explore! Enrich!
